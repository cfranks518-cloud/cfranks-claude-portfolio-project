Welcome to

OutfitGen

Your personal digital closet. Upload your clothes, then let OutfitGen pick your outfit for the day.

1

📷 Take a photo of your clothing

Photograph your T-Shirts, Pants, and Shoes — one item at a time. Keep each photo clear with good lighting.

2

+ Add Item — Upload & describe it

Tap + Add Item in the top right. Upload your photo, choose the type (T-Shirt, Pants, or Shoes), and fill in the color, brand, and size. You can add up to 5 items per category.

3

✨ Generate your outfit

Once your closet has at least 1 shirt, 1 pants, and 1 pair of shoes, tap ✨ Generate Outfit and OutfitGen will pick a random combination just for you. Tap Regenerate to keep shuffling!

💡

The items you see now are EXAMPLE clothes already loaded so you can explore. You can delete them anytime and add your own.
